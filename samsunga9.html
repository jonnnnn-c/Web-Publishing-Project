Brand new Samsung Galaxy A9


The all new 4 Quad Camera

World's first 4 lens smartphone

We understand how important it is for your perspective to be captured, we designed the first quad lens camera smartphone to show you we heard you. Pictures taken are sharp and mesmerising on the Galaxy A9.

Capture more with the power of four

Take gorgeous photos without compromise using any combination of the Galaxy A9’s Ultra Wide Camera, Telephoto Camera, 24MP Main Camera, and 5MP Depth Camera. For even more pizzazz, apply intelligent software features such as Scene Optimizer and Flaw Detection to transform your shots from beautiful to breathtaking.

Fancy colours to suit your style

Pick a colour that suits your style


Available in 3 colours – Caviar Black, Bubblegum Pink, and Lemonade Blue – enhanced by premium glass-like casing and sleek design, treating yourself to a product experience that is both polished and irreverent.

Infinite viewing pleasure


Immerse yourself in captivating Galaxy A9’s expansive 6.3” FHD+ Super AMOLED Infinity Display. Producing impressively sharp contrast and beautifully vibrant colour, you’ll be able to see the world through your screen – as it truly is.

Always available when you need it


Kept up to speed on important updates with Always-on-Display, featuring information such as the time, date, weather alerts, incoming notifications – without having to wake your phone up.

Light as a feather

Say good bye to heavy smartphones as the all new Galaxy A9 weighs only at 183g.

Cutting-edge Performance

Feel the power of A9


Enjoy high performance powered by an advanced mobile Octa-Core processor with 6GB RAM, allowing you to do a variety of activties - watching videos, playing games, snapping pictures.

Store more, Enjoy more


Comes with 128GB of internal memory to store your memories. If that is not enough, it has a microSD storage of up to 512GB.

To still be updated on work when you're away on a holiday, Galaxy A9 comes with dual SIM to give you that flexibility.

Charge fast, last longer

Comes with a USB Type-C compatible charging port and a large 3800mAH battery. The "juice" and the speed of charging to get you through your day.

Face Recognition


Unlock your Galaxy A9 almost immediately with Face Recognition technology.

Fingerprint Scanner


Access your Galaxy A9 with the reliable fingerprint scanning that enables you to unlock your phone and also log in to websites and apps with ease.
